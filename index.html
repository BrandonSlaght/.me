About Me

Currently a 21 year old junior at the University of Rochester, working on my B.S. in Computer Science, leaning towards the Human-Computer Interaction track. My summers are spent in Cleveland, Ohio.

I have taken many rigorous classes in native languages, systems, theory, and math, but my passion lies in creating experiences, and as such I have also taken classes in front-end web development, digital art, and Android development. A proud co-owner of BAD Media, which currently satisfies my creative side.

I have taken on ambitious side projects and self-taught languages for them. In the past summers I have interned at Sherwin-Williams creating internal facing websites for one of their research facilities, and at Progressive Insurance adding features to, redesigning, and migrating pages from ASP to MVC on a business critical website.
