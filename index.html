About Me

Currently a 20 year old junior at the University of Rochester, working on my B.S. in Computer Science, leaning towards the Human-Computer Interaction track. My summers are spent in Cleveland, Ohio.

I have taken many rigorous classes in native languages, systems, theory, and math, but my passion lies in creating experiences, and as such I have also taken classes in front-end web development, digital art, and Android development. A proud co-owner of BAD Media, which currently satisfies my creative side.

Kinnect Slouch tracking

Code
Paper (pdf)

My group successfully managed to detect whether a user seated at a desk was slouching, leaning, or otherwise had bad posture with a high degree of accuracy. To accomplish this, we analyzed three-dimensional input from a Microsoft Kinnect and matched the normalized data with a premade, categorized set of measurements using a K-NN classification system.
